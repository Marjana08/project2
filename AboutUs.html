About Us

Saver Life is a voluntary institution of medical and dental students of Bangladesh, works mainly on Safe Blood Transfusion . Saver Life started its journey on 5th February,1977 by some medical students with the motive of doing things for humanity. Saver Life has become a great social icon in Bangladesh with its own activities since birth. In this long period, Saver Life has very successfully arranged voluntary blood donation programs.
